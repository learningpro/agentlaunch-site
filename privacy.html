AgentLaunch Privacy Policy

AgentLaunch is a fully local application that does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. All your data stays on your device.

Network Access

AgentLaunch does not require network access. It only launches terminal applications with your configurations. The CLI tools you configure (such as Claude Code or Codex) may have their own network requirements — please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Local Storage

All settings and profiles are stored locally using SwiftData. No data is uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

AgentLaunch integrates with CLI tools (Claude Code, Codex, and custom tools) that you configure. These tools operate independently, and their privacy policies apply to their respective services:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us via the App Store listing.
